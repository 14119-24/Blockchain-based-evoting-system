Privacy Policy

Data Protection and Privacy

1. Data Collection: We collect only necessary information for voting.

2. Data Encryption: All personal data is encrypted using industry standards.

3. Identity Protection: Your national ID is hashed and never stored in plain text.

4. Vote Anonymity: Your vote is separated from your identity using cryptographic hashing.

5. Blockchain Storage: Only encrypted votes are stored on the blockchain.

6. Data Retention: Personal data is retained only as required by election laws.

7. Security Measures: We implement multiple security layers to protect your data.

8. Your Rights: You have the right to access and correct your personal information.

Your privacy is our priority in maintaining election integrity.
